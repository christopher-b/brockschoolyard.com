The Brock Yard Revival fundraising campaign has officially closed, and we're thrilled to report that our community raised **$128,703.62**.

What started in 2022 as a request to the TDSB has grown into the largest infrastructure project in our school's recent memory, made possible by hundreds of families, neighbours, local businesses, and friends of Brock who contributed to the project in many ways.

## What happens now?

The TDSB construction process is underway. We're expecting shovels in the ground this summer, with the goal of students stepping into their new schoolyard when school opens in September 2026.

We'll be posting updates here as construction progresses: when work begins, milestone photos, and a first look at the finished space. If you want to stay in the loop, bookmark this page.

To everyone who donated, volunteered, showed up, spread the word, or simply cheered us on: thank you.
